Opened in 2012, Deora Concept is a shared working environment designed for individuals and organizations looking for a turnkey office solution that offers flexibility and value without compromise.

About

The idea is simple: a cool, functional workspace solution for people who need a place to set up shop or for established companies looking to grow. Deora is Irish-Gaelic for "drops" — important on their own, but stronger together. Our concept is to create an environment where like-minded individuals and organizations can work in a space that offers all of the amenities and conveniences of a larger office at a fraction of the cost, while also creating a community that offers the opportunity for collaboration with others.
